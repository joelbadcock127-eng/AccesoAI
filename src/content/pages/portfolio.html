02 Case Study No.1

The website that runs a table tennis club.

Devonport Table Tennis Association · live fixtures, results and ladders

Devonport Table Tennis Association is a volunteer-run club in North West Tasmania. Like most local sporting clubs, it ran on goodwill and spreadsheets. The season draw lived in a document, results were typed up and passed around by hand, ladders and player averages were worked out manually each week, and most of it depended on a couple of dedicated volunteers keeping everything straight.

Acceso AI's founder Joel is also the club's treasurer, so this one was personal. The club needed a professional website, but a custom system like this would normally cost far more than a small club could ever justify. Instead, the whole thing was built with AI. Joel described what the club needed in plain language, and AI wrote, tested, and refined the code in short, iterative batches. No software team. No agency budget.

The result is one central place that runs the club. The full season draw is published online, results are entered once and the ladders and player averages update instantly, members can log in to their own dashboard, and the committee manages the whole competition from a single admin page.

Before

  • Season draw kept in spreadsheets and printouts
  • Results typed up and passed around by hand
  • Ladders and averages recalculated manually each week
  • Club knowledge held by a couple of volunteers

After

  • Results entered once, everything updates instantly
  • Player averages and history available to everyone
